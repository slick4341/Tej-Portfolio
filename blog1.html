The Rarest Windows Shortcuts You Probably Don’t Know

Posted 10 July 2025

While many users are familiar with classics like Ctrl+C and Alt+Tab, there exists a hidden world of Windows shortcuts that can supercharge your workflow. These rare gems are seldom used, yet incredibly powerful.

Mastering these combinations not only saves time but also uncovers features you didn’t know your operating system had. Here are a few of the rarest Windows shortcuts worth trying.

“Productivity is never an accident. It is always the result of a commitment to excellence, intelligent planning, and focused effort.”

Below are some shortcuts that even seasoned Windows users might not have encountered:

  • Win+Ctrl+D — Create a new Virtual Desktop instantly.
  • Win+Ctrl+F4 — Close the current Virtual Desktop.
  • Win+Pause/Break — Open System Properties directly.
  • Ctrl+Shift+Esc — Open Task Manager
  • Win+. — Open the Emoji & Symbols picker.

These shortcuts are not only rare but also incredibly practical once you make them part of your daily habits. Take a moment to try them out and see which ones enhance your productivity the most.
